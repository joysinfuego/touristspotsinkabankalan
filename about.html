Our Story

Unveiling the Treasures of our City Welcome to the enchanting city of Kabankalan, where every street, every corner, and every smile tells a story of vibrant culture and natural wonders. Our About Page is designed to be your gateway to the rich tapestry of tourist spots that make Kabankalan a destination like no other.

Our Story: Nestled in the heart of Negros Occidental, Kabankalan is a city that proudly celebrates its cultural heritage while embracing the breathtaking beauty of its surroundings. Our story is one of resilience, warmth, and a deep connection to the land that has shaped our identity.



Natural Wonders: Kabankalan is home to a plethora of natural wonders that will captivate your senses.
